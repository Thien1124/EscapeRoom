## 🧪 1. SCIENCE LAB - "Thí Nghiệm Bị Ngắt Quãng"

### 🎨 **Theme:** Cold, sterile laboratory
**Colors:** Cool whites, blues, cyan accents
**Mood:** Clinical, technological, interrupted experiment

### 💡 **Lighting System:**
- 🌟 **Ambient:** Cool blue hemisphere light (0xa3d4ff / 0x0f1a28)
- 💡 **Fluorescent Strips:** 4 overhead RectAreaLights (0xddeeff)
- 💠 **Scanner Glow:** Cyan point light (0x00ffff)
- 🔬 **Microscope Light:** Bright white spotlight for workspace
- 🚨 **Emergency Strips:** Blue safety lights on walls (0x3388ff)
- 🌫️ **Fog:** Cool blue fog (0x0a1628)

### 🏗️ **Decorations:**
- 🏢 **Industrial Ceiling:** White metallic panels (0xf0f8ff)
- 💡 **4 Light Strips:** Glowing fluorescent emissive boxes
- 🧪 **3 Lab Stations:** Clean white workbenches with collision
- 💻 **2 Computer Terminals:** Black monitors with green screens (0x00ff88)
- 🧫 **4 Specimen Containers:** Transparent cylinders with colorful specimens
- 🌪️ **3 Ventilation Units:** Metallic air vents on walls
- ⚠️ **Warning Signs:** Yellow panels with red glow (hazard symbols)

### 🎨 **Materials:**
- **Floor:** Shiny lab floor (0x5a6d7e, low roughness 0.3)
- **Walls:** Clean white-blue (0xd4e4f7, metallic 0.15)
- **Equipment:** High metalness for professional look
